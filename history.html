History

Notes on the Club’s origins and milestones.

1885
Origins of a dining club for London hospital administrators and chief officers.
1896
Club impedimenta acquired; traditions established around dinners and speakers.
2016
Regular dinners resumed with programmes hosted in hospitals and clubs.
Today
A network for current and former health CEOs and chief officers.
